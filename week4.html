5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

Code2College is an organization that promotes STEM fields to underrepresented groups through education and mentorship. People with programming knowledge can volunteer there to teach highschool students how to code, as well as various business and leadership skills that will equip them for success. It's a really cool effort and definitely something worth looking into if anyone is interested in that sort of thing.
